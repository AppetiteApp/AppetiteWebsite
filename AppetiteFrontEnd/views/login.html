Appetite is a social platform for people to buy and sell the meals that they prepare in their own kitchens

The App

Browse by location for a meal that sounds good to you. Select one that sounds good and you will be brought to a page detailing price, ingredients, and the time it is ready. Click order when you're ready to purchase your dish!

If you are selling a meal, simply fill in the form, wait for someone to order, and get cooking!
